Welcome to this
12th of August, 2020

Many trips around the sun ago, I learned about the code that holds together the virtual-reality that society obsesses over.

Despite since spending countless hours maintaining the cluttered back-alleys that serve the DOMs and HTTP requests, I had never enacted upon my youthful desire to have a corner of the Internet that I could call truly my own.

This website aims to fulfil that naive desire.

As such there are some motivations behind this site:

  1. It will aim to be light-weight, low-fuss, and low-tech, with a focus on functionality overall.
  2. Reliance on external service providers will be minimised, for the sake of DIY attitude and learning experience.
  3. Current social media is not beneficial, and shall be kept as far away as possible.
  4. Creating > consuming.
  5. Doing > not doing.
  6. Connections > broadcasting.
  7. I am alive and in a constant state of flux.

Much like myself, this site not yet knows what it is.
It will gradually find it's way.
